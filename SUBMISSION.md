```text
Unstuck Coach is a folder-based whole-person executive-function accessibility coach for people whose bottleneck is not intelligence or effort, but the moment life asks them to start, switch, remember, regulate, capture, recover, or close loops without shame. Optional shorter version:
```

```text
Unstuck Coach is an executive-function accessibility coach for whole people whose lives stall at the executive-function layer. It helps with task initiation, home/admin loops, inbox and calendar management, choosing one next action, holding context, idea capture, communication-threat spirals, transitions, hyperfocus recovery, and shame-free re-entry after avoidance.

The key move is coaching over information: state check first, one concrete next action, zero-friction capture, visible time, and no productivity lectures when the user needs external executive function.
```
